<div class="rules-container">
  <h1>Server Rules</h1>
  <p>To keep the Community First experience enjoyable for everyone, please adhere to the following guidelines:</p>

  <h2>1. General Conduct</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Be respectful to other players. No toxic behavior or harassment.</li>
    <li>No hate speech, racism, or sexism of any kind.</li>
    <li>Listen to Staff instructions. Their word is final.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>2. Gameplay & PVE</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>No Griefing: Do not intentionally ruin the experience for others.</li>
    <li>Stealing from other players' bases is strictly prohibited in PVE zones.</li>
    <li>Do not block major roads or high-tier loot spawns with permanent structures.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>3. Exploits</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>No glitching, hacking, or using exploits to gain an unfair advantage.</li>
    <li>Report any bugs found to the Discord staff immediately.</li>
  </ul>

  <div class="warning">
    ⚠️ Failure to follow these rules will result in a warning, kick, or permanent ban.
  </div>
</div>
